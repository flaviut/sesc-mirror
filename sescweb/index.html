Welcome to the SESC simulation environment homepage

SESC is built in the Department of Computer Science at Urbana-Champaign in the IACOMA group.

SESC Sourceforge home page

Another simulator??? Choice is good. Although Simplescalar and RSIM are wonderful simulators, I believe that Simplescalar is having too much weight in the computer architecture research community. SESC tries to provide an alternative to well known simulators.

We expect that most of the people would use the simulator for their "papers". In that situation, they would be reluctant to update the cvs server with the latest information about their work. Our proposal for that situation is the following:
  • You can work freely with SESC as long as you want. Do not commit or report modifications while you are working in your paper.
  • Once you have published a paper, if and only if you want, send us the simulator extensions and we will post them in the web-site
If you have any suggestion/code to improve the performance or the simulation accuracy contact us. Personally, I would like more people actively working in the simulator (see MAINTAINERS file).
