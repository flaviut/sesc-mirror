Mailing List

Some of your questions may already be solved in the mailing lists. sesc-news is intended to have low traffic, and emails are posted only when something relevant happens in sesc. sesc-talk is supposed to have all the sesc bugs and proposals emails.
  • sesc-news@lists.sourceforge.net (info) (archives)
  • sesc-talk@lists.sourceforge.net (info) (archives)
This two mailinglist are managed with mailman, therefore you can also send emails to sesc-news-request@lists.sourceforge.net and to sesc-talk-request@lists.sourceforge.net. Send an email with the keyword help in the subject too see the available commands.
